St. Patrick's Cathedral

About St. Patrick's Cathedral.


St. Patrick’s Cathedral is situated on Shahrah-e-Iraq near the Empress Market, Saddar Karachi, Pakistan. It is the first church in Sindh province, Pakistan. It was initially built on the grounds of this cathedral in 1845, and was called St. Patrick’s Church. In April 1881 the present cathedral was opened.
